Gold Theft Trap

Objectives

  • Each player starts with 1 gold coin
  • Every time you select ‘Gold’, your Gold counter increases by 1.
  • For a basic win: You must collect 5 gold coins.
  • A basic loss happens when you have 0 gold coins.

Win/loss variations

  • If you play Gold and P2 plays Theft, not only does P2 gain 1 gold, you lose 1 gold as well.
  • You can choose to steal P2’s gold via Theft, but beware that P2 can play Trap and win the whole game.
  • You can, however, anticipate that P2 will play Theft, and play Trap to win it all.
  • Trap, when played agains Trap, achieves a null outcome.
